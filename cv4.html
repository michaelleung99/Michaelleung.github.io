    This page is dedicated to food! I mean, Who doesn't love food? During my most recent trip to New York with my friends, we purposely went to The Big Apple so we could try different foods from different cultures. We had a select variety of foods like sushi, chicken on a waffle cone, italian pizza, classic breakfast sandwich, lobster, and a Beijing street snack called Jianbing.
